Nature Therapy Walks

healing in harmony with nature

Walking with Awareness

Nature therapy walks combine the healing power of nature with mindful movement and therapeutic dialogue. These sessions offer a unique opportunity to:

  • Connect deeply with the natural world
  • Practice mindful movement and presence
  • Process emotions in an open, natural setting
  • Find metaphors and wisdom in nature
  • Experience embodied healing

Session Format

Nature therapy walks are typically 90-120 minutes, conducted in carefully selected natural environments. Each session includes:

  • Initial grounding and intention setting
  • Guided mindful walking
  • Therapeutic dialogue and reflection
  • Nature-based exercises and observations
  • Closing integration practice

Practical Information

Sessions are available year-round, adapting to seasonal changes. Comfortable walking shoes and weather-appropriate clothing are recommended. All fitness levels are welcome, as we adjust the pace and terrain to suit individual needs.
