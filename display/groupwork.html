Group Sessions

growing together in community

Collective Growth

Our group sessions create a supportive environment where shared experiences become catalysts for personal transformation. Through facilitated dialogue and structured exercises, participants have the opportunity to:

  • Learn from shared experiences and diverse perspectives
  • Develop deeper interpersonal awareness
  • Practice authentic communication
  • Build meaningful connections
  • Gain insights through collective wisdom

Group Format

Groups typically meet weekly or bi-weekly for 2-hour sessions. We maintain small group sizes (6-8 participants) to ensure meaningful interaction and personal attention. Current group offerings include:

  • Personal growth and awareness circles
  • Mindfulness and meditation groups
  • Emotional intelligence workshops
  • Life transition support groups

What to Expect

Each group session is carefully structured to include:

  • Opening check-in and grounding practice
  • Themed discussion or experiential exercise
  • Shared reflection and feedback
  • Integration and closing practice
